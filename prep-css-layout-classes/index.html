Indiana Jones

A thrilling entertaining movie directed by Steven Spielberg and not only co-written, but co-produced by George Lucas in 1981. Following along our main protaganist Indiana Jones through old ruins riddled with traps in search of archaeological treasures and fight off his competitors the Nazis.

After the success of the previous movie, Steven Spielberg directs the second installment in the Indiana Jones franchise, which is actually the prequel to the first movie. However, the Indian Government prevented them from shooting due to Spielberg refusing the changing of the script. Thus moving production to Sri Lanka and London, but also getting the film banned from India after completion.

The third installment of the thrilling Indiana Jones franchise. After his father goes missing, when searching for the Holy Grail, Indy uses his father's notebook to find clues on his whereabouts. He is lead to Venice, where he finds assistance from Dr. Elsa Schneider, but is then met with sinister old enemies who are also after the Holy Grail.
